epic supporters news news blog newsletters resources resources resources ? members only nextgen cto program overview program highlights benefits application key dates overview the nextgen cto program aims to develop the next generation of ctos (chief technology/technical officers). ctos are important executive-level positions especially in innovation-driven companies as they lead the technology department and oversee the development and innovation of new products and services. ctos must understand the importance of intellectual property (ip) and how ip can be leveraged strategically to grow their businesses. participants will gain an understanding of various forms of ip and develop ip savviness that will give them a competitive advantage in their future careers, whether as a cto at their own startup or for other organizations. this program is open to students from all faculties, as well as business owners, entrepreneurs, and like-minded people in our community. we invite anyone who wants to learn more about leveraging intellectual property in their projects and businesses to apply. program highlights throughout the 5-week program, participants will learn key skills, competencies and know-how through a carefully selected combination of: online learning modules designed to provide an accessible learning environment that fits the participant?s schedule virtual workshops with engaging speakers lectures led by specialists in intellectual property group discussions to diversify participant knowledge case studies that show ip strategy in action participants are expected to complete assigned online modules before weekly webinars delivered by distinguished speakers. weekly webinars will take place every friday from 1:00 to 2:30 pm. benefits upon completion of the program, participants will have: gained an understanding of different types of ip learned various ways to leverage ip in business strategy learned how different ip can interact received training on ip licensing as well as ip protection and enforcement participants will also receive: access to centre for international governance innovation online learning modules opportunities to interact with notable specialists in intellectual property a certificate of completion and micro-credential badge that can be acknowledged on resumes, linkedin, and business webpages access to the epicentre network
