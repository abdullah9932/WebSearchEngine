you have reached this page because you are conducting research at h?tel-dieu grace healthcare. university of windsor's research ethics board serves as the oversight board for all research conducted at h?tel-dieu grace healthcare. here you will find the full complement of forms to be completed and submitted to the research ethics board at the university of windsor: research ethics forms which forms do i complete? procedural steps in applying to conduct research with h?tel-dieu grace healthcare: download the university of windsor application package and required documents. submit the completed package to the university of windsor reb along with a letter of no objection and tcps2 core certificates for the principal and co-investigators. after receiving clearance, submit protocol and updated forms including the clearance and letter of no objection to hotel dieu. h?tel-dieu grace healthcare application package requirements application packages submitted need to include: letter of no objection additional forms these forms may be required as you conduct your research: progress report (yearly update for active research) final report (end of study) request to revise (changes to research) adverse events & serious adverse events (tba) contact the uwindsor office of research ethics if you have any questions or require further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.
