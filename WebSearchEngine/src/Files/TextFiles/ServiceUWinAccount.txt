uwinid uwinid is a string that uniquely identifies you on our network. it appears in front of the "@" sign in your university of windsor primary email address (uwinid@uwindsor.ca). your primary uwindsor e-mail address is used to sign in to most of university computer systems. changing uwinid it is generally not permitted to change your uwin id except under special circumstances. for more information please see this knowledge base article. uwin password users generate their uwin password when they activate their uwin account. the uwin password must be sufficiently complex and must meet several guidelines that are outlined in this knowledge base article: what are uwin account password guidelines? all users are required to change their password at least once every 365 days. if the password is not changed before it is set to expire, uwin account will be locked and it can only be unlocked by changing the password. users who forgot their password can reset it by using account.uwindsor.ca (available 24/7) or by contacting the it service desk (during university business hours). to use account.uwindsor.ca, your security questions must be configured prior to the first use. for more information, please see these knowledge base articles: what to do if i forgot my uwin account password?
