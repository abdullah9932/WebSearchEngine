centre archives & special collections writing support desk paul martin law library connect with your library spaces and bookings covid-19 service notice: jan 31st library hours will be extended. masks are mandatory when in common spaces in leddy library even while seated. eating is not allowed in the library. questions, or having trouble reserving a room because everything is booked? please contact the information desk. policies for study spaces during covid-19 all users entering the library are required to complete the covid-19 self assessment using the safe lancer app (available for download on apple app store or google play store). as of septmeber 7th the campus vaccine policy is in effect. safety protocols and other information will be sent via email upon reservation. please read this carefully before attending. all study rooms are single occupancy only including rooms formerly zoned as group study. masks are mandatory and physical distancing guidelines will be in effect when in the leddy library. the library is not zoned as a cafeteria where you can take your mask of while you are eating. supplies will be provided for wiping down your study space before?and?after use. no food or drink will be permitted, except for lidded drinks. study rooms are highly requested. your booking will be canceled if you do not arrive within 30 minutes of the start of your booking. personal and group study rooms personal study rooms are available for individual use and can be booked for two 3 hour sessions a day, up to 30 days in advance. reserve a study room computer workstations pc workstations provide you with access to printing and a workspace for studying. available first come, first served. study spaces study carrels and tables are available on a first-come, first-served basis. both west and main buildings are open. access data computing reserve sessions to connect to a remote workstation and use a variety of statistical and gis software. access remote computing
