university of windsor is located on the canada-united states border in the heart of north america's automobility, design and manufacturing industries. our unique location and partnerships provide students with opportunities to contribute to leading-edge research and gain unparalleled international study and work experience. uwindsor has a long history of contributing to major automotive innovations and supporting the commercialization of new technologies that have made vehicles safer, smarter and cleaner. we maintains close ties with major auto companies and suppliers such as stellantis (formerly fiat chrysler automobiles), ford motor company and general motors canada. the university has a dynamic commitment to the environment ? we're also situated in the great lakes region between lake erie and lake huron on the shores of the detroit river. as the first canadian university to offer a degree in environmental engineering, we?ve built a thriving team that boasts internationally recognized researchers with expertise in renewable energy, energy storage, clean water and clean air technologies. learn more about our graduate programs master of engineering (meng)
