accessibility map & information campus map uwindsor.ca academic programs admissions scholarships student life residence life scoreboard financial support lancer varsity athletics attracts exceptional student-athletes at the highest level in their sport and promotes the merits of goal setting and team achievement on and off the field. the university of windsor and our lancer athletics program prides itself on offering the best student experience possible to our student-athletes. we are committed to helping students with demonstrated financial need manage the education expenses associated with pursuing your university degree (tuition, books and mandatory course fees). financial assistance is available through a variety of sources including osap, scholarships, bursaries, and our ignite work study program. scholarships, awards and bursaries are available through the office of student awards and financial aid (https://www.uwindsor.ca/studentawards). all uwindsor students must fill out the uwin award profile to be eligible for consideration for any financial award or bursary. blue and gold athletic scholarships we understand that earning an athletic scholarship can be a game-changing opportunity for student-athletes. your combined success and dedication in both academics and athletics can score you much needed financial support and allows the university of windsor to attract outstanding student-athletes from across canada, the united states and internationally. as such, the department of athletics is pleased to provide blue and gold athletic scholarships to incoming and returning lancer student-athletes. under ontario university athletics guidelines, varsity teams in ontario are able to allocated athletic financial awards up to a maximum of $4,500 per year/athlete to qualified student-athletes in addition to other academic awards and bursaries to help offset expenses for the school year. who is eligible? to qualify for blue and gold scholarships, entering student-athletes must have a minimum academic average of 80% to be eligible. to continue the award, returning student-athletes must maintain a minimum 70% average in each academic year. how do i apply? to apply for a blue and gold athletic scholarship at the university of windsor, please contact the head coach of your sport. for a complete list of our head coaches, please click here. how do i donate to an athletic scholarship? to learn more about how you can make a difference by making a tax-deductible donation to an athletic scholarship of your choice, please contact mona sleiman at
