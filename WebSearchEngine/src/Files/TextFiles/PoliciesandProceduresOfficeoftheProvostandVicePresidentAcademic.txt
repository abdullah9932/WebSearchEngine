procedures policies and procedures explanatory message in spring 2018, the university of windsor established an ad hoc planning committee to develop a central policies index. this repository launched in summer 2019 intending to better organize and grant access to many of the institution's general policies for faculty, staff, and/or students. in time, it will include policies that emanate from the board of governors, senate, senior administration, and other arms of the university of windsor. access the central policies index at uwindsor.ca/policies. as that site is still being populated, the
