research partnerships industry engagement industry engagement we are committed to building collaborations between industry, research, and education to transform ideas into products, competitive advantages, and a deeper understanding of our world. our research experts are motivated to create solutions to challenges faced by industry which consists of developing or testing proof of concepts, developing innovative prototypes or services and improving existing production and performance methods. our office can assist with: connecting you to highly qualified researchers and other research partners leveraging funding opportunities to complete research projects assisting with licensing university research and technologies providing access to state-of-the-art research equipment and
