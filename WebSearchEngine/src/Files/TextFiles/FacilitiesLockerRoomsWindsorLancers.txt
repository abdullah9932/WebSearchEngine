locker rooms locker room etiquette the university of windsor lancers athletics & recreational services staff are committed to offer you the best service, and we ask for your assistance in making our locker room a safe & clean environment. please monitor your children at all times while in the locker room & shower area. only children under the age of four (4) years old may accompany parents of the opposite sex into the locker room. help keep a clean environment for everyone by using the trash bins provided, and by reporting any spills or leaks to the service desk. lockers are for day use only. locks left on day use lockers overnight will be removed. please do not use your cell phone camera within the locker room & shower area. food, gum, beverages, & glass items are prohibited within the locker room & shower area. the university of windsor is not responsible for lost or stolen articles. lock all valuables in your locker, check the lock security before leaving the locker room, and do not give your locker combination to anyone. report suspicious behavior to the service desk. anyone found defacing or damaging this locker room and/or shower area is subject to disciplinary action including suspension of privileges, and potential termination of your membership and will be held financially responsible for repairs. if this room requires custodial or maintenance services, please advise the staff at the service desk. sponsors footer terms of service, opens in new window privacy policy, opens in new window sidearm sports learfield, opens a new window ad blocker detected thanks for visiting ! the use of software that blocks ads hinders our ability to serve you the content you came here to enjoy. we ask that you consider turning off your ad blocker so we can deliver you the best experience possible while you are here. thank you for your support! learn more about ad blockers close
