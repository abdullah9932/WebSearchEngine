new exam information for instructors covid-19 update: new exam information for instructors (winter 2022) student accessibility services will only be invigilating in-person exams for sas students registered in hybrid and face to face courses that are holding in-person exams all exams being completed remotely will not be invigilated by sas. students have been advised to evaluate their accommodation needs for tests, exams, etc. listed on revised syllabi they receive from you. student accessibility advisors are available o assist you and your students as to how best to accommodate. we also have this online video advising instructors on when they may wish to consult with our office. assessments that are not timed will eliminate the extended time accommodation that students have administered through our office and in a regular exam setting. if you will be scheduling online timed exams, individual settings will have to be set up based on the length of time listed in the accommodation letters e-mailed to you. in most cases, students requiring the use of access software will have these on their home computers. (note: they may not have used this software in the online environments for exams.) students with condition flare-ups may require extensions. some students have restrictions of one exam per day. if you are teaching courses with no in-person exams, you will be able to view a current list of sas students and their accommodations through the aim faculty portal: https://olympic.accessiblelearning.com/uwindsor/ if a student has scheduled to write an exam in the sas office, you will receive an email with instructions regarding submitting the exam. sas advisors listed on the accommodation letters should be contacted to walk through questions about any accommodations needed\ ***in the event a student is rescheduling a quiz, test, midterm or final exam as per their accommodations, we will need all faculty to fill out this form and send it to exam@uwindsor.ca and thank you for your support and cooperation: https://www.uwindsor.ca/studentaccessibility/sites/uwindsor.ca.studentaccessibility/files/change_of_exam_form_alternate_day_rescheduling_fillable_2.pdf contact information: general: sas@uwindsor.ca exam: exam@uwindsor.ca advisors: anthony gomez - gomez1@uwindsor.ca christine quaglia - cquaglia@uwindsor.ca joyceln lorito - jlorito@uwindsor.ca david soderlund - david.soderlund@uwindsor.ca share tweet save share tweet save contact us university directory accessibility news services for media uwindsor mobile apps terms & conditions policies manage uwin account emergency services for students follow us on twitter follow us on facebook follow us on linkedin follow us on flickr follow us on youtube follow us on instagram
