diploma programs faculties and departments university of windsor academic programs faculties and departments faculties and departments for a mid-size university, the university of windsor likes to think big. we offer more than 120 majors and minors and 70 master's and doctoral degree programs. once you choose your degree, the bulk of your core classes will fall within one of our nine academic divisions, called faculties: faculty of arts, humanities and social sciences faculty of education faculty of engineering faculty of graduate studies faculty of human kinetics faculty of law faculty of nursing odette school of business faculty of science the university is also home to the schulich school of medicine and dentistry - windsor campus. some faculties are home to smaller academic departments, where you'll have the opportunity to work closely with a tight-knit group of professors, graduate students and classmates. faculty of arts, humanities and social sciences (including anthrozoology, digital journalism, social justice, bachelor of interdisciplinary arts and science) communication, media, and film dramatic art english, language, literature and creative writing
