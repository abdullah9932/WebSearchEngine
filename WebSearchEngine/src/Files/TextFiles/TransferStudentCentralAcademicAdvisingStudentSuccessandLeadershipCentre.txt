we can assist you with general support regarding how to read your academic progess report, course registration, changing majors and introducing you to the university of windsor's policies and procedures. coming to a new institution can be challenging and it is essential to your academic success that you utilize our campus student support services. i encourage you to meet with an academic advisor every semester to ensure that you are on the right track, utilize professor?s office hours, and get involved on campus through lead@uwindsor or by joining a club! when you have some free time, be sure to explore windsor-essex county and all it has to offer, or hop across our boarder to explore detroit, michigan! star-gaze at point pelee national park, enjoy some italian food on erie street, explore our biking and hiking trails, attend a concert at casino windsor or little caesars arena, purchase tickets to a detroit tigers game, and explore world-class galleries and museums! throughout your undergraduate experience, if you have any questions, feel free to stop by the advising office. we are located in dillon hall, room 111 (lower level). sincerely, central academic advisor - transfer student support share tweet save useful links course add form: special circumstance request declaration of second degree/certificate form request for letter of permission to take course(s) at another university for transfer credit form alternative final examination(s) due to conflict with religious conviction form alternative final examination(s) due to 3 exams scheduled on the same day form transfer student policy course translator fahss academic advisors student account drop page 2021 uwinsite registration guide fall 2021 course delivery modes share tweet save contact us university directory accessibility news services for media uwindsor mobile apps terms & conditions policies manage uwin account emergency services for students follow us on twitter follow us on facebook follow us on linkedin follow us on flickr follow us on youtube follow us on instagram
