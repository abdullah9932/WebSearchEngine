library think you would make a great part of our team? join us! the university has a vaccination policy in place. per the policy, after october 21, 2021 all uwindsor employees, students, contractors and visitors must be fully vaccinated, or, partially vaccinated and awaiting eligibility for their second dose, or have an approved exemption from the vaccination policy to be able to attend campus. any individuals who must attend campus are required to wear a non-medical mask when entering all campus buildings and in common areas where physical distancing is difficult. self-screening prior to arrival on-campus through the safe lancer app is mandatory. for additional information, please visit return to campus webpage. student jobs we hire students to work at the computer help desk as student consultants. student consultants help library patrons troubleshoot issues with the library's computers. to apply, please fill out the student consultant application and submit your completed application along with your resume by email to ledsys@uwindsor.ca. the subject of your email must be: yyyy-mm-dd - leddy consultant application (where yyyy-mm-dd is the date of submission). library staff positions please check the university of windsor's daily news on a regular basis. librarian positions please visit the faculty recruitment page on the provost's website for librarian postings.
