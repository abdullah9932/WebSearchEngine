"celebrating champions on the field, in the classroom and throughout the community" alongside our corporate partner the investors group, the department of athletics at the university of windsor is pleased to present the ig champions cup. the ig champions cup is a department trophy that's awarded each year to the lancer varsity team that best exemplifies what it takes to be a champion in the classroom, on the playing field and throughout the community. lancer varsity teams gain points towards the ig champions cup throughout the year based on a variety of contributing factors including team athletic success, academic achievements, individual conference and league accolades and team community involvement initiatives. the points breakdown for the ig champions cup is as follows: factor points awarded conference finish 1st place 40 points, 2nd place 35 points, 3rd place 30 points, 4th place 25 points, 5th place 20 points conference improvement 10 points for each position a team moves up in the standings from the previous year. cis and oua team championships cis championship ? 30 points, oua championship - 20 points individual accolades (note: all-canadians and all-stars points awarded up to a max of 3 per team) cis athlete of the year - 40 points, cis all-canadian 25 points, cis academic all-canadian 25 points, oua athlete/player of the year - 25 points, oua all-star 20 points, oua or cis rookie of the year 15 points, cis or oua athlete of the week 10 points, oua- team of the month 10 points, oua or cis all-rookie team 10 points, uwindsor athlete of the week 5 points team academic success (note: points awarded based on final first semester marks) team average over 80% gpa ?
