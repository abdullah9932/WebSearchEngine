your library deposit your research share and preserve your research in scholarship at uwindsor ? the university's institutional repository. this service is available to faculty and students and satisfies policy requirements from the university of windsor, the tri-agency funders (nserc, sshrc, cihr), and other organizations. you may choose to deposit your research yourself, or have us do it. for questions or comments about this service, please contact us at scholarship@uwindsor.ca. do it yourself you can deposit your own research using the scholarship at uwindsor website. follow these steps: go to scholarship at uwindsor and sign in with your uwin account and password hit submit research under author corner and select the department you want to upload to fill out the submission form with as much information about your article as possible and upload the appropriate version of your article. submit your article will then be reviewed and posted by an administrator note: after submitting your materials a preview screen will appear. read through all of the information carefully, checking for errors. you will also receive an email confirming your submission. this email will also give you a link to check the status of your paper and make revisions until it is publicly posted. let us do it or, you can ask the library to deposit your research. to do so: send an email to scholarship@uwindsor.ca with the following information: bibliographic information about your research an appropriate version of your article. check your email. we may be in touch to clarify some points before we publish. connect with your library contact us research & reference help library hours my library account wellness blackboard manage uwin account terms and conditions emergency
