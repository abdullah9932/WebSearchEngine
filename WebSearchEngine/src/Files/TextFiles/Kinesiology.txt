is home to two majors of study, kinesiology - movement science and sport management & leadership. both programs now allow for direct entry into their respective majors within the faculty of human kinetics. students in both will receive an honours degree in human kinetics upon successful completion of their studies and will join the rich history of alumni who are making their mark in fields such as kinesiology, chiropractic care, medicine, sports, recreation, business and teaching, just to name a few. click on one below to explore it in more detail. explore kinesiology - movement science
