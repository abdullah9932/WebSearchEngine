we want to help you realize your professional potential here in canada. the university of windsor, in collaboration with three other ontario universities, is part of the ontario internationally educated nurses course consortium. the ontario internationally educated nurses course consortium has developed a hybrid model competency-bridging program of study to help prepare internationally educated nurses (iens) to launch their registered nursing careers in ontario. more information about the competency-bridging program of study can be found here https://rncompetencies.ca/. applications open on the website until may 2, 2022. this program will put you on a path to potentially become a registered nurse (rn) in ontario! interest list if you would like to be notified about future offerings of this program, sign up for the continuing education interest list and we will contact you with further details. program information and application portal can be found on the rn competencies website at rncompetencies.ca additional helpful links for internationally educated nurses: college of nurses of ontario (cno) - for nurses educated outside canada national nursing assessment service internationally educated nurses competency assessment program share tweet save share tweet save contact us university directory accessibility news services for media uwindsor mobile apps terms & conditions policies manage uwin account emergency services for students follow us on twitter follow us on facebook follow us on linkedin follow us on flickr follow us on youtube follow us on instagram
