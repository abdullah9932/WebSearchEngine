faculties and departments university of windsor academic programs academic programs at the university of windsor, you'll join an academic community that's fully invested in your success, whether you're a full-time undergraduate or graduate degree candidate or a part-time certificate or continuing education student. as a mid-size university supporting 16,000 students, uwindsor provides a broad range of academic opportunities. as an undergraduate or graduate student, you can choose from over 120 majors and minors and more than 70 masters and doctoral degrees in subjects like engineering, forensics and business. at the heart of all of our programs is our exceptional faculty, men and women who are leading scholars in their fields and dedicated mentors in the classroom. learn more
