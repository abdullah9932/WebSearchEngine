a place for undergraduate science collaborative and integrative experiences. this interdisciplinary network brings together faculty and students from all science programs to work collaboratively in providing a unique integrative support system with the goal of enriching the undergraduate student experience. located in room 335 in essex hall, the usci network is a dedicated space for experiential learning with a flexible classroom, a hands-on laboratory, and a student meeting room. creating a network of science students, faculty and staff to deliver resources and provide integrative opportunities to enrich the student experience. connect to the usci network through any of the following student-lead initiatives: destination science careers in science science meets art women in science sci of relief science living learning community stemxpolicy usci network benefits of engagement: builds your professional and social networks with students, staff and professors enhances your collaborative skills strengthens your communication skills builds your management skills enriches your undergraduate experience gain job ready skills qualify for service learning course credit earn your lead medallion if you are a faculty of science student interested in volunteering for the usci network please complete this volunteer interest form for more information, please contact usci@uwindsor.ca check out our usci network photos follow us on facebook, instagram, and twitter!
