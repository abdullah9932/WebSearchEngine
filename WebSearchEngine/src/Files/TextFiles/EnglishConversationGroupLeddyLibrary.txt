english conversation skills this semester? the sessions will take place online using blackboard collaborate from thursday, jan. 20, to apr. 7. no registration is required, just stop by and say hello: when: thursdays at 12 noon, jan. 20 to apr. 7 where: online via blackboard https://bit.ly/leddyecg2022 we encourage you to self enroll in our ecg organization site to receive updated information on our activities and to access all our english conversation group resources about library services, english improvement, and other weekly topics. to self enroll, please login to blackboard first in another tab, and then access the following link which should self enroll you into the ecg organization site:
