epic team epic board of advisors epic impact epic supporters news news blog newsletters resources resources resources ? members only entrepreneurship speakher series overview key dates season 1 - now season 2 - upcoming overview funded by the us consulate general office, the entrepreneurship speakher series is a virtual speaker and networking series that aims to empower post-secondary students, especially women, to consider entrepreneurship as a potential career path. the series will include 8 virtual webinars featuring american woman entrepreneurs from various industry sectors. there will also be two virtual networking sessions providing opportunities for students from university of windsor, st. clair college and other institutions in windsor essex region to network with students studying at universities in the united states. the objectives of the entrepreneurship speakher series are: to motivate and encourage post-secondary students, especially women, to discover the opportunity within the realm of entrepreneurship, regardless of their fields of study. to strengthen the bilateral ties between canada and the united states by uniting woman entrepreneurs with potential entrepreneurs from both nations. to provide value to our speakers and audience by providing an open platform for networking and communication during each webinar to foster new connections! this series is free for participants to attend! key dates this series will be run over two semesters, winter 2022 and fall 2022. each semester, there will be 4 webinars featuring woman entrepreneurs from different industry sectors and one virtual networking session. the following schedule is tentative. the confirmed session will include a registration link. we encourage you to check back frequently for updates! season 1 sessions (winter 2022) ?
