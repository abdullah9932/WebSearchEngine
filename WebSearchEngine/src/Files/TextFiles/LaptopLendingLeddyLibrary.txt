order from other libraries finding items in leddy tech & accessories your library account connect with your library laptop lending page banner the library has 30 chromebooks available for loan at the library's information desk. all users must read and sign the laptop lending agreement form before they can participate in the lending program. to learn how to get started with your chromebook, view our quickstart guide. check availability laptop lending policies policy details eligible users active university of windsor students, faculty, and staff with no outstanding fines or charges over $20. lending agreement form patrons must read and sign the leddy library laptop lending agreement to be able to participate in the service. this will be added to their patron record. standard loan period 24 hours. please let us know in advance if you might need the laptop for a longer period. renewals when you return your laptop to the desk, if other laptops are ready and available, yes. if not, wait 1 day. late fees $5 per hour until status changes to lost. lost laptops 48 hours after the laptop is due, it will be considered lost. at that time a replacement fee of $375.00 for the laptop and $30.00 for the charger (if applicable) will be added to your account. appeals fines may be appealed, with good reason, to the following persons in the order given: access services coordinator head, access services (in writing only) associate university librarian / university librarian (in writing only) dean, student affairs (please contact the dean?s office for procedures) connect with your
