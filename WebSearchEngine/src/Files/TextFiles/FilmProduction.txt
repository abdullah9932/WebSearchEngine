production (bfa) develop your visual, technical, storytelling, and professional workplace skills. study all aspects of filmmaking in scripted and documentary film including pre-production concept development, screenwriting, project planning, budgeting; production management, directing, cinematography, set design, lighting, sound; and post-production including sound design and editing. learn scripted, fiction, documentary, and experimental filmmaking traditions and create films working in close-knit teams with peers. the bfa, film production program is a joint program offered by the department of communication, media & film (cmf) and the school of creative arts (soca). admissions: bfa, film production program: min avg 70% ontario course requirements: eng4u required first year entry only. link to uwindsor's future students website: film program highlights and more application information. link to ontario universities' application centre (ouac): https://www.ouac.on.ca/
