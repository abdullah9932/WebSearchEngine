contact us uwindsor home chemical control centre hazardous waste management hazardous waste management the university of windsor's chemical control centre has developed several hazardous waste management programs to assist the campus community with the safe handling of hazardous waste and to ensure that all wastes generated on campus are handled in accordonace with regulatory requirements and in a manner which minimizes adverse effects on health, safety, and the environment. learn more about hazardous waste programs in addition, the chemical control centre is responsible for providing training, information, and advice pertaining to chemical safety, hazardous materials handing, disposal, storage, spill response/reporting and environmental management. contact the chemical control centre if you have any questions or require further information, please do not hesitate to contact us. chemical control centre office: b37 essex hall phone: (519) 253-3000 ext. 3523 fax: (519)
