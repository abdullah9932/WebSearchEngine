reporting treasury treasury mission & vision treasury supports the overall administration of investments and cash management for the university. in accordance with university financial policies and procedures, as well as our statement of investment policies and procedures, the treasury department is responsible for: investments borrowing cash flow needs of the institution. the above responsibilities involve monitoring: the working capital endowment and pension investment portfolios investment performance oversight reporting to government bodies pension plan financial reporting obtaining external financing. the treasury department also oversees the internal loans of the university. credit rating the university is rated annually by two credit rating agencies, moody?s and
