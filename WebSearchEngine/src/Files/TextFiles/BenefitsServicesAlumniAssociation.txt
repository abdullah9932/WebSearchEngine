information contact us uwindsor home alumni association benefits & services benefits & services a number of benefits and services are available to university of windsor alumni and students as well as faculty and staff. visit the links below for details about the benefits and services currently available to association members and their families. alumni membership card & privileges alumni mastercard home & auto insurance life & health insurance choice hotels diploma framing alumni email services hire a uwindsor student career services for alumni alumni discounts the alumni association forms partnerships with organizations to provide many of these services. the selection and approval of these corporate partners is undertaken by a volunteer committee of alumni and students who evaluate and review each proposal on the basis of the following: benefit to the members (alumni, students, faculty & staff) product value and quality stability of organization impact on association and university resources in addition to providing services and products to our members, corporate partnerships also generate revenue. as a direct result of alumni participation in these affinity services, your alumni association is able to fund student scholarships and awards programs and numerous other initiatives in support of students and alumni. read about our commitment to protect your
