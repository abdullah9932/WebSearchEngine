women's and gender studies wgst dance fitness for students wgst dance fitness for students tuesday, april 19, 2022 - 16:00 women's and gender studies presents dance fitness with jolie katembo jolie katembo is a local dance choreographer/creative business entrepreneur who is passionate about empowering women through fitness and artistic expression. with a theatre performance degree from st. clair college, she has worked on numerous productions for art's collective theatre and other local dance events. jolie's most recent endeavour is sister-sister fitness, an online and in-person fitness group that facilitates experiences where every woman is uplifted and encouraged towards a healthier mind and body. join us for a free session of dance hall fusion with hiit fit! tuesday, april 19, 2022 4:00-5:00 p.m. education gym, student centre, university of windsor register online please dress appropriately for exercise (comfortable clothing, running shoes) and bring your own water bottle. all participants will be required to sign a waiver prior to participation. you may participate at your level of fitness and do not require previous experience with dance fitness. covid-19 safety protocols must be followed, wearing masks and practicing social distancing, and all attendees are required to provide proof of double vaccination via the safe lancer app (see https://www.uwindsor.ca/returntocampus/469/attestation-and-providing-proof) or by providing proof on site. danielle reaume wgst@uwindsor.ca 5192533000 extension: 2315 share tweet save get social with
