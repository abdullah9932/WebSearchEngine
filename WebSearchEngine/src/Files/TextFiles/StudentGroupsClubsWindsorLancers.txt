online by clicking here. lancer varsity competitive clubs men's baseball the university of windsor lancer baseball team is a member of the national club baseball association (ncba) competing against 170+ other universities and colleges from across the united states. windsor competes in the ncba division one and is in the great lakes north conference. windsor also competes at the ontario university association (oua) tournament each fall against 13 other ontario universities. head coach: frank jeney contact: fjeney@hotmail.com, 519-990-5510 women's fastpitch the university of windsor lancer fastpitch team is a member of the ontario intercollegiate women's fastpitch association (oiwfa) competing against 13 other ontario universities each fall. head coach: paul scott contact: pscott@uwindsor.ca, 519-792-7294 lancer recreation competitive clubs lancer competitive dance company our team is a dedicated group of students who share the passion of dance, all having different experiences with dance in the past. this is our 5th year on campus. our captain vanessa randazzo along with her 4 co captains work hard in making this team the best experience possible. we prepare approximately 8-10 dances to compete in out of town competitions. we compete against every other ontario university in the gta, in march representing the university of windsor. lcdc also showcases in february to windsor. we are looking at a team of approximately 35-40 dancers this year. it is a great opportunity to be part of a team, meet new people, and pride yourself in being a lancer. student
