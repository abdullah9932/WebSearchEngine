links communications contact us events human resources return to campus - what aau heads need to know return to campus - what aau heads need to know the following resources, developed in partnership with the office of the provost, have been designed to support aau heads as the university continues to plan a safe and gradual return to campus activities. 1 - view the video - return to campus - what aau heads need to know this video presentation provides an overview of : roles and responsibilities of deans and aau heads with respect to return to campus planning. responsibilities of aau heads as supervisors of staff and student employees which include: some student and faculty return to campus information communication reminders for having supportive conversations. resources for staying informed as well as wellness supports (this video is 38 minutes in length) after watching the video complete the acknowledgement for found below. 2 - complete the acknowledgement form select the link below to complete the online acknowledgement form. after successful completion, you will receive an email certificate. please keep this email certificate for your records. please note - the acknowledgement form refers to the return to campus: what supervisors need to know as the aau head module is recorded in our system as a supervisor module. acknowledgement form should you require any assistance, please contact the health & safety team at safety@uwindsor.ca or at extension 4547. 3 - download/view the resource documents return to campus - what aau heads need to know - this resource document includes the slides and transcript from the video. download the resource document return to campus - what supervisors need to do - as an aau head, you have supervisory responsibilities with respect to staff and student employees in your department. this resource outlines some key actions and provides links to key resources. download the resource document share tweet save share tweet save
