recreation mission the mission of lancer recreation is to support student well-being through engagement in recreation and leadership opportunities to foster a lifetime commitment to personal development. vision we will provide current and innovative recreation programming to enrich the university of windsor student experience principles and values (p.l.e.a.s.e.) partnership: build sustainable relationships with campus, community, and global organizations to support equitable, diverse, and accessible opportunities for student lifelong learning on and off campus. leadership development: provide opportunities for students to evolve as leaders by providing opportunities for all students to develop leadership training, experiential learning objectives and career competencies. equity/diversity/inclusivity: support a welcoming and inclusive environment that values and offers opportunities for the diverse qualities of all people including gender, sexual orientation, language, age, ability status, national origin, religion, socioeconomic status, as well as race, ethnicity, and heritage. accessibility: create an environment that values, embraces and enriches individual differences by providing programs, services, and staff which reflect the diversity of the university. sustainability: meet the present and future recreational needs of the university of windsor campus and community without compromising the ability of upcoming generations to meet the social, economic, and environmental challenges of the future. engagement: develop a lancer and community-wide awareness of our programs and services to attract and inform new and current participants of recreational opportunities. location: st. denis centre building - dennis fairall
