overview social entrepreneurs play a critical role in community development and social betterment in local and global communities across the globe. in partnership with libro credit union, our libro-epic social enterprise program aims to support changemakers looking to launch and grow a social enterprise through specialized coaching, network boosting, tailored workshops, and explorative opportunities to test and build their business model. successful applicants can expect to receive: $2,000 in funding + the opportunity to pitch at the end of the program to win cash prizes totaling $6,000 access to exclusive workshops, learning seminars, and speaker sessions with distinguished guest speakers 1:1 tailored coaching and business support through pillar nonprofit access to epicentre co-working space opportunity to work alongside like-minded social entrepreneurs this program is valued at over $7,000 and is 100% free of cost to all participants. eligibility you have a basic understanding of social entrepreneurship you have an impactful vision and understanding of the social and/or environmental problem you are trying to solve and a potential solution your business idea is a for-profit or non-profit social enterprise your revenue (or potential revenue) from the sale of products or services funds a significant part of your business model the business is (or will be) based in windsor-essex the business is 2 years old or less. the business can be early-staged as long as you have the goal of earning revenue during the 16-week program you are available for the mandatory sessions listed in key dates this program will also be delivered through a hybrid approach of virtual sessions as well as in-person activities. these activities will take place at the university of windsor epicentre. all applicants applying to this program must comply with the university of windsor?s return to campus policies. more information can be found at https://www.uwindsor.ca/returntocampus/ program highlights the libro-epic social enterprise program is specifically designed for aspiring social entrepreneurs who are driven by a blended value proposition of financial, social, and environmental or cultural returns. through this program, you will receive tailored coaching, specialized resources, tools, and networks to help you build a thriving social enterprise. throughout the 16-week program, participants will have the opportunity to attend workshops and learning sessions on social entrepreneurship fundamentals and business model development. program participants can also expect to: be connected with an experienced and dynamic social enterprise coach have access to epicentre?s expert advisory panel pitching opportunities with cash prizes available network with social entrepreneurs, local leaders, and like-minded people benefits social enterprises selected for this program will: have access up to $2,000 in funding on eligible business expenses. these expenses are typically used towards marketing and promotion, accounting and legal services, product development, and other support pitch their idea to a panel of judges at the end of the program for a chance to win the $5,000 grand prize, as well as $1,000 people?s choice award have free access to epicentre?s co-working space during the program + 1-year
