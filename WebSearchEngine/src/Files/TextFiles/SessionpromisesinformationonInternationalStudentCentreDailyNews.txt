webinar at noon wednesday, april 6. session promises information on international student centre apr 5th, 2022 uwindsor faculty and staff wanting to better help international students can learn how during an information session at noon wednesday, april 6. the international student centre presents know your isc on microsoft teams. learn about its services and programs for students in english language improvement, undergraduate, graduate, or post-graduate programs. the international student centre accompanies clients through every stage of their lancer journey, beginning with specialized orientation at the start of every term, the university health insurance program, immigration and visa advising, student exchange opportunities, and more. as more international students choose the university of windsor for their post-secondary educations, it is important to provide adequate support to those who may be unfamiliar with canadian ways of operation. the presentation will take about 30 minutes, with time afterward for questions. register here to attend. international student centre strategic priority: promote international engagement share tweet save external job postings mar 30, 2022 - full time lead hand - starbucks coffee shop feb 23, 2022 - graduate/ financial secretary '8' in the department of electrical & computer engineering jan 14, 2022 - facilities / equipment / events technician classification "iv" in the department of athletics & recreational services internal job postings mar 31, 2022 - work-integrated learning coordinator ?
