changes to isolation requirements and testing centre hours released on march 30, 2022 march 30, 2022 changes to isolation requirements based on current federal, provincial and local trends, the university of windsor is aligning with public health directives for positive and/or presumed cases and close contacts. effective monday, april 4, 2022, the university of windsor will move to the provincial standard of five-day isolation for positive and/or presumed covid-19 cases and certain close contacts as outlined in provincial guidance. illness reporting for students, faculty and staff is required via email to covid19reporting@uwindsor.ca. specific instructions regarding campus return date for individuals who have reported covid-19 related illness, close contact or travel will be provided directly as part of this process. the guidance for covid-19 positive, presumed positive and close contacts document outlines important steps when reporting an illness. screening through the daily self-assessment questionnaire (safe lancer app) continues to be required. new hours: covid-19 testing centre beginning monday, april 4 the covid-19 on-campus testing centre will be open by appointment only during the following hours. monday: 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. tuesday: 1:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. wednesday: closed thursday: 8:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. friday through sunday: closed questions? email returntocampus@uwindsor.ca
