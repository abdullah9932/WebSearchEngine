15,000 students enrolled in a broad range of undergraduate and graduate programs. when you become a university of windsor employee, you join a community of talented and dedicated individuals whose knowledge, skills and abilities enable the university to: provide students with an educational experience that will serve them for a lifetime. carry-out scholarship and research that reveal new
