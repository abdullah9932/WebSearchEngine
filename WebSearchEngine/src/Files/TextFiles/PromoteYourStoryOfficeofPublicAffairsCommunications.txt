uwindsor image library uwindsor home office of public affairs & communications news services promote your story promote your story you have a great story to tell. your research and creative activity is important to the university of windsor and plays a key role in supporting its strategic priorities. when you share your stories with the greater community you help the public understand the vital work uwindsor undertakes to make a better world through education, scholarship, research and engagement. the office of news services, public affairs & communications (pac), wants to help you reach the right audience at the right time with the right message. getting the word out on what you are doing can take many forms and depends on what you want to say, why you want to say it, and who needs to hear your message. allow pac to help you reach your optimal audience. please contact: lori lewis manager, news services 519-253-3000 ext. 3241 llewis@uwindsor.ca we will get in touch with you and
