age cancer survivors childcan volunteer tutoring program for school age cancer survivors volunteer tutors urgently needed offered at no charge to children who are returning to school after undergoing cancer treatment, the childcan volunteer tutoring program helps students to catch up on what they missed during their treatment. volunteer, and gain job-related experience by helping a child to become more successful. a volunteer tutor is needed for a grade 8 child in windsor who is in need of some extra one-on-one support as she prepares for grade 9. some experience and interest in working with children with learning needs would be helpful. the tutor works with the child in the family home, usually once a week, on a flexible schedule. contact: jackie kinder, vtp coordinator, for more
