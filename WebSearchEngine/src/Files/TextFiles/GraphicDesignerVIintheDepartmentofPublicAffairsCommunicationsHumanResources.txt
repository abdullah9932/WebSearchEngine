hr quick links communications contact us events human resources graphic designer "vi" in the department of public affairs & communications graphic designer "vi" in the department of public affairs & communications employment group list: cupe 1393 posting type: internal and external job posting number: 2022-13-19 closing date: 08/04/2022 - 4:00pm primary job responsibilities: reporting to the manager of public affairs and communications, the graphic designer provides design and creative services for information vehicles developed by the department of public affairs and communications (pac). the position assists pac in maintaining a consistent visual identity in print materials, websites, multimedia, photography, illustration etc., through design standards, guidelines, seminars, templates etc. the graphic designer will also assist in the design and production of promotional electronic communications in support of the university of windsor?s goals and objectives. essential qualifications: the successful candidate will possess: a university degree or college diploma in graphic design at least five (5) years of experience in print publication development and execution, including art direction and project management three (3) years of experience directly related to web and/or electronic publication design and development background in photography and photo editing and knowledge of print techniques and processes advanced skills are required in adobe indesign, adobe bridge, adobe photoshop, adobe illustrator, adobe acrobat professional, fontbook, microsoft excel, microsoft word, powerpoint, lotus notes, and html an understanding of multi-media applications including dreamweaver ability to conduct press checks. must be highly creative with good communication skills and be able to work under pressure preferred qualifications demonstrable skills in addressing functionality issues relating to the web, especially accessibility for the disabled; advanced skills in flash and multimedia software, as well as experience with database systems; registered graphic designer designation. *applicants must submit a portfolio that includes 10 samples of web and/or electronic publication design, as well as 10 samples of print design. rate of pay: classification "vi" per schedule a of the collective agreement how to apply if you need an accommodation for any part of the application and hiring process, please notify the employment coordinator at extension 4523. should you require further information on accommodations, please visit the office of human rights, equity & accessibility (ohrea). please note that the job postings are removed from this site by the end of business on the closing date listed on the posting. the university thanks all applicants for their interest, however, only those candidates considered for an interview will be contacted. applications submitted through employment agencies will not be considered. share tweet save
