Description

McMaster SafetyApp is the official safety app of McMaster University. It is the only app that integrates with McMaster's safety and security systems. McMaster University has worked to develop a unique app that provides students, faculty and staff with added safety on the McMaster campus. The app will send you important safety alerts and provide instant access to campus safety resources.

McMaster SafetyApp features include:

- Emergency Contacts: Contact the correct services for the McMaster area in case of an emergency or a non-emergency concern

- Tip Reporting: Report a safety/security concern directly to McMaster security.

- Safety Notifications: Receive instant notifications and instructions from campus safety when on-campus emergencies occur.

- Campus Maps
- Campus Map: Navigate around the McMaster University area.
- Crime Mapping: View recent crimes that have occurred on and near campus.

- Campus Safety Resources: access all important safety resources in one convenient app.

Download today and ensure that you’re prepared in the event of an emergency.
