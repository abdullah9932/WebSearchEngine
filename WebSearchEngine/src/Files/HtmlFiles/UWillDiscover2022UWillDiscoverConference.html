UWill Discover 2022

Welcome to the home of the UWillDiscover Conference at the University of Windsor!

UWill Discover 2022 will be a 4-day event (March 29th to April 1st) in which undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Windsor can present their research, scholarship, and creative work.

Submissions are now open (Submit proposal via link in tool bar). Also, don't forget to following us on instagram or twitterfor more updates!
