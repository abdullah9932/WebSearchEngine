Undergraduate

Click to view the University's COVID-19 Info Page

The emphasis in the Bachelor of Human Kinetics degree is on providing students with an understanding of human physical activity in its broadest sense, rather than upon the details of professional practice and teaching methodologies.

A Bachelor of Human Kinetics degree may be earned either through a regular, eight-term program of study, or through the twelve-term Co-operative Education Program, which combines classroom and related work term experiences. Students are able to specialize in one of two honours Majors: Kinesiology - Movement Science or Sport Management & Leadership. 

Majors: 

Sport Management & Leadership

Admission Requirements: 

  • 4U Math
  • Minimum grade of 70% in 4U English
  • Minimum average of 70% in top six 4U courses

Kinesiology - Movement Science

Admission Requirements: 

  • Minimum grade of 70% in one of 4U Biology, Chemistry or Physics
  • Minimum grade of 70% in Grade 4U English
  • Minimum average of 70% in top six 4U courses

For additional information regarding our Undergraduate Program, please contact: tmdale@uwindsor.ca
