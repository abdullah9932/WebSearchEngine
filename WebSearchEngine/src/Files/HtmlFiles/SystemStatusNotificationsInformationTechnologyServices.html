System Status & Notifications

Impending Google Services Retirement

UWindsor Google accounts will be deleted soon. You may need to migrate or download files by April 30, 2022, if: 

  • You are a student or alumni who received an email listing failed files after the 2020 migration process. 

  • You are a student or alumni who used Google products like Sheets or Docs, which saves to Google Drive, after the migration process. 

  • You are a student or alumni who used the Google account for storage after the migration process. 

  • You are a faculty and staff member with a UWin Gmail account and have files saved on Google Drive. 

  • You are an alumni who used Gmail services between 2008 and 2020, and have not maintained your @uwindsor.ca account in good standing and wish to reactivate it.  

Find information on how to migrate, download files, or reactivate your alumni email account: uwindsor.ca/itservices/google-services-retirement

Updated: Feb 18, 2022 - 9:12 am
Apply iOS Device Patch Immediately to Macs, iPhones and iPads

A vulnerability, CVE-2022-22620, has been found on iOS devices. Users of iOS, iPadOS, and macOS devices are advised to apply the latest operating system patches immediately. The vulnerability affects all web browsers and apps that use web browsing in the background. It allows Apple devices to be instantly infected with malware by visiting a web page or viewing a malicious advertisement on a safe website. 

 

See Apple Support for the patch. https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT213093

Updated: Feb 01, 2022 - 3:36 pm
Security vulnerability for SPSS users
SPSS CLIENT INTERIM FIX INSTRUCTIONS
IBM remediation for this security vulnerability is found in this IBM Tech Note with instructions and downloads to interim fixes: https://www.ibm.com/support/pages/node/6525830

Please note that before the interim fix (Fixpack) can be applied, the SPSS version must be fully patched. For example, if you have SPSS Statistics 27.0 deployed, you must first update it to Statistics 27.0.1 before applying the associated interim fix.

SPSS CONCURRENT LICENSE MANAGER UTILITY
SPSS Concurrent License Manager utility is not affected by this vulnerability. IBM SPSS leverages Sentinel RMS software and the developer has released a statement that confirms their infrastructure, applications, products, and services aren’t vulnerable to the CVE-2021-44228 exploit.
https://www.sentinelone.com/blog/cve-2021-44228-staying-secure-apache-log4j-vulnerability/

For additional information, here is a link to IBM’s general update on the Apache Log4j CVE-2021-44228 vulnerability:
https://www.ibm.com/blogs/psirt/an-update-on-the-apache-log4j-cve-2021-44228-vulnerability/

For SPSS support, please contact spss@orion.on.ca. An ORION rep will respond to your inquiry within a half-business day Monday to Friday 9 am to 5 pm Eastern, excluding public holidays and limited support during Christmas-New Years.
