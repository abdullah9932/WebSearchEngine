In Store Travel Clinic

Start planning six to eight weeks ahead to protect yourself against new illnesses

For some destinations (e.g., India, Africa), it might take even longer to prepare.

Protect yourself by:

  • Learning about the place youre travelling to
  • Following health precautions specific to that place  see the Public Health Agency of Canada website
  • Getting immunized against diseases, when possible
