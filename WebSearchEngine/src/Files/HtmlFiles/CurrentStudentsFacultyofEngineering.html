Current Students

As an engineering student at UWindsor, you’ll be surrounded by classmates, mentors and faculty who share your passion for engineering, innovation and research. We encourage you to take advantage of our experiential opportunities to learn and grow as an individual and future engineer. 

Do you have questions about your #UWindsorENG experience?
Our Engineering Student Support Services Centre has the answers!
 

Engineering Student Support Services Centre

The Engineering Student Support Services Centre is your one-stop shop for academic and wellness support. Receive academic guidance, tutoring, counselling, writing support and much more.
