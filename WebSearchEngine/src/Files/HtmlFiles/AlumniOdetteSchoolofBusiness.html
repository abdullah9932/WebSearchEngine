Alumni

We have many opportunities for alumni to “give back” and help our students launch successful careers as well as drive the Odette Business School to the next level.

Opportunities for alumni include:
  • Mentoring Students
  • Guest Speakers
  • Leader for a day
  • Odette Advisory Board
  • Case Competition Judges
  • Hire an Odette Student
  • Attending Events

To learn more about these opportunities, contact Barbara Barone at (519) 253-3000 ext. 3678 or bbarone@uwindsor.ca.
