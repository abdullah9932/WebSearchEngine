Forms

The REB has launched a revised application form for research ethics review. Along with minor revisions in wording and repitition, the form includes two major changes.  First, Section 25 aligns with the new Tri-Agency Research Data Management Policy https://science.gc.ca/eic/site/063.nsf/eng/h_97610.html

Second, the revised form incorporates the questions previously contained on the On-line checklist and the separate on-line checklist form will no longer be required.

The new form will become mandatory for use on January 1, 2022.

Please click here to view the form.  

If you have additional Research Ethics questions or concerns, please contact the Office of Research Ethics or the Research Ethics Board Chair at ethics@uwindsor.ca.  

Note: If you are using the UWindsor Psychology Participant Pool to conduct your study, the REB asks that you consult with them prior to obtaining clearance at psycpool@uwindsor.ca.
