Career Services Office

The Career Services Office can help you determine or refine your career objectives, navigate the various recruitment processes, assist in the preparation of your application packages, and help you research traditional and non-traditional opportunities.

Hours & Services

Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:30am  - 12:00 noon and 1:00pm- 4:30pm.

Appointments with the Director of Career Services, Anna Maria DeCia-Gualtieri, are available from 10:00 am - 12:00 noon, and from 2:00 pm - 4:00 pm, with some exceptions. 

Appointments exploring alternative career options are available with the Social Justice Career Coordinator, Katie Behan. 

Appointments working on resume and cover letter writing, mock interviews and more are available with the Career Advisor, Selena Randhawa.  

We offer:

  • individual career coaching
  • programs and workshops
  • resume and cover letter clinics
  • peer mentors, job shadowing and,
  • an extensive resource library.

Simplicity

Use "Symplicity", our Career Services Management System, to access our employer database, job postings, events, and for program/recruitment scheduling.

Current students do not register as a new user as you already have system access.
If you misplace your username and/or password, email Deirdre Charron.

>>Symplicity Log In

For Employers

If you want to arrange on-campus interviews for a job opening or are interested in attending the annual Windsor Law career fair, email Anna Maria DeCia-Gualtieri, Director of Career Services, at adecia@uwindsor.ca.
