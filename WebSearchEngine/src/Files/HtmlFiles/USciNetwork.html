USci Network

Welcome to the USci Network! A place for Undergraduate Science Collaborative and Integrative experiences.

This interdisciplinary network brings together faculty and students from all Science programs to work collaboratively in providing a unique integrative support system with the goal of enriching the undergraduate student experience.

Located in Room 335 in Essex Hall, the USci network is a dedicated space for experiential learning with a flexible classroom, a hands-on laboratory, and a student meeting room. 

Creating a network of Science students, faculty and staff to deliver resources and provide integrative opportunities to enrich the student experience. Connect to the USci Network through any of the following student-lead initiatives:

  • Destination Science
  • Careers in Science
  • Science Meets Art
  • Women in Science
  • Sci of Relief
  • Science Living Learning Community
  • STEMxPolicy

USci Network Benefits of Engagement:

  • Builds your professional and social networks with students, staff and professors
  • Enhances your collaborative skills
  • Strengthens your communication skills
  • Builds your management skills
  • Enriches your undergraduate experience
  • Gain job ready skills
  • Qualify for Service Learning Course Credit
  • Earn your LEAD Medallion

If you are a Faculty of Science Student interested in volunteering for the USci Network please complete this Volunteer Interest Form

For more information, please contact usci@uwindsor.ca
