Contact Graduate Studies

gradst@uwindsor.ca
If you are a student, please provide your full name and student number in your email so we may better assist you.

Services by Graduate Studies continue by: 

  • Email at gradst@uwindsor.ca
  • Telephone at 519-253-3000 ext. 2109; Monday through Friday 9:00 am to noon.
  • Students may make an in-person appointment to speak to a member of the Graduate Studies team by emailing gradst@uwindsor.ca, Monday to Friday between 9:00 am to 4:00 pm. (Please note that you may not receive an appointment the same day that you email your request).

For the latest information about the University of Windsor's response to the COVID-19 pandemic, visit UWindsor Together: Return to Campus Life and Community.

Office hours:

Monday to Friday: 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.

Visit Our Office:

The Graduate Studies Office is located on the third floor of Chrysler Hall Tower.
