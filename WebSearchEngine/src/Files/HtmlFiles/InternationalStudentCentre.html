International Student Centre

Welcome to the International Student Centre (ISC) at the University of Windsor! 

Connect with us!

   

The doors to the ISC are now open for limited in-person services, Monday through Friday from 8:30 am - 4:30 pm. 

If you are planning to travel to Canada, please review the information on the COVID-19 Travel Guide.

If you are a newly arrived student, please email isc@uwindsor.ca and gship@uwindsor.ca for further assistance.  A virtual Softlanding session is available for new students via Blackboard.  For all other matters, please connect with us via email or Blackboard.

UPDATE: The University of Windsor is approved to reopen to international students from outside of Canada.

UWindsor is on the approved list of designated learning institutions (DLIs) that have reopened to asymptomatic international students from outside of Canada. International students who have been approved for a study permit can now travel to Canada for their studies.

Please click on the blue bar below for general information and travel updates.

International Arrivals New and Returning Students

 

The International Student Centre continues to provide support and services as follows:

  • ISC Blackboard Live Virtual Services (current students only -check for times through the ISC Blackboard page)
  • General inquiries related to International Student services and supports: isc@uwindsor.ca
  • Green Shield (OHIP Equivalent Health Coverage) gship@uwindsor.ca
  • Student Exchange exchange@uwindsor.ca
  • Study Permit, VISA and Immigration questions: intladvisors@uwindsor.ca
  • Tax questions (from February until April 30 of each year): isctax@uwindsor.ca

Many resources are also available for current students through ISC’s Blackboard site.  If you can't find the answers you're looking for on our website, on Blackboard or by using the ask.uwindsor.ca site, please visit the ISC Virtual Front Desk!  You can access the Virtual Front Desk through our Blackboard site by clicking on "ISC Virtual Lounge" in the left-hand menu.  We are happy to help in any way we can!

Attention new students: please complete the Soft Landing Arrival Form if you haven't already done so.
