Updates to COVID-19

The Sexual Misconduct Office is still open for appointments via phone or video chat. You can reach Dusty at svsupport@uwindsor.ca if you’d like to talk.
We’re also still active on social media! Follow our facebook or instagram for information about self care and wellness, resources, and activities.

REPORT AN INCIDENT

 


 

Get Support

If you have experienced sexual misconduct or violence you deserve help and support. If you aren’t sure what to do next, click here for more information. Your voice and your choices matter.

 
 


 

Give Support

Has someone disclosed an experience that you think might qualify as sexual misconduct? We can offer information on how to provide support.
