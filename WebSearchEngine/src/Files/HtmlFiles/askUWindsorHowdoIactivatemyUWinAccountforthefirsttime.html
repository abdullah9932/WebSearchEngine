How do I activate my UWin Account for the first time?

Your UWin Account is your personal digital identification for accessing several online services at UWindsor. Applicants need to activate their UWin Account prior to accessing UWinsite Student (undergraduates) or eGAS (graduates).  
 
To activate your UWin Account: 

1. Go to the First-time Student/Applicant Account Activation page.

2. Enter your Student Number and PIN. (Your Student Number and PIN are in your Application Acknowledgement Email from the University of Windsor.)

3. Click the Submit button.
 

 
4. Your UWin ID will be generated. Please write it down.
 
5. Create your new Password. 
 
6. Proceed to the next step.
 

NOTES:

  • If you are applying to an undergraduate program at UWindsor, you will want to proceed to UWinsite Student after activating your UWin Account. In UWinsite Student you can respond to items in your To Do List – including uploading your documents – and monitor the status of your application(s). If you need assistance logging in to UWinsite Student, please refer to this ask.UWindsor.ca article. As an applicant, you will need to use the Applicant Homepage in UWinsite Student. If you see the My Homepage or the Student Homepage, click the little white arrow beside it and select “Applicant Homepage” from the drop-down menu.
  • If you are applying to a graduate program at UWindsor, you will want to proceed to eGAS after activating your UWin Account. In eGAS you can upload your required documents, finalize the submission of your application and monitor its status. Please remember you must wait at least 24 hours from the receipt of your application acknowledgement email before trying to access eGAS.
