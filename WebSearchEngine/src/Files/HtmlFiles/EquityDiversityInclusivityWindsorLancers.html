Black History Month 2022 Events Calendar

There will be a showing of The Loyola Project documentary on Sunday, March 13th. The Loyola Project focuses on the 1963 National Championship Loyola Ramblers who changed the game of college basketball during the height of the civil rights era and overcame numerous racial challenges and blatant inequalities, on the road to becoming champions on the court and in the community. It will occur in person at the Armouries in the Performance Hall from 1-3pm (by invite only). Basketballs with the University of Windsor logo on them will be distributed to youth who attend, sponsored by Lancer Men's Basketball. The following Thursday, March 17th we will have an online showing of The Loyola Project, open to the University Community, from 1-3pm (as part of KINE2300 Sociology of Sport class). Sign up here. Attendance to both sessions is free!
