Faculty and Staff Directory

At Windsor Law, our greatest asset is our people. Our faculty are exceptional scholars and excellent teachers who are among the leaders in their fields. They are widely published in books and prominent law journals, and are actively engaged in community and public policy issues.

Together, they form a vibrant intellectual and social community that provides our students with an appreciation of law as a vehicle for social and legal change.
