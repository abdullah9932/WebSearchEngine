How to Apply

Step 1

Apply to ELIP

You can apply to the English Language Pathway Program through the link below.

Apply Now

Step 2

ELIP or ELIP 3 Fast Track

Select ELIP or ELIP 3 Fast Track (ELIP 3 Fast Track eligibility based on valid standardized test score: i.e., IELTS overall 6.0 + writing band of 6.0; or TOEFL minimum score 75 + writing score of 18 or greater. Please contact applyesl@uwindsor.ca if you are not sure about your eligibility).

Step 3

Add to Cart

Select term and then Add to Cart

Step 4

Checkout

Proceed to Checkout. You will receive an email with account activation and profile completion instructions shortly after.
