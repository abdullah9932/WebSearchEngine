International Open Day

Our UWindsor International Open Day is over but all of UWindsor’s presentations will be available in the Auditorium for you to watch until July 5, 2021. You can also browse our many booths, save resources to your virtual backpack and connect with us by chat at ask.uwindsor.ca

Login with your email to access the event and resources.

Visit and you will be able to:

  • Watch our webinars, and find out about programs, awards, financial aid, student support, and academic advising
  • Learn about your programs of interest by browsing the program booths and watching sessions in the auditorium
  • Connect with the recruitment team and staff through chat in the booths
  • Find out about scholarships, financial aid, residence and how to apply

We hope you will find getting to know our campus (virtually) a fun experience!

Contest Rules & Restrictions

  1. To be eligible to win a UWindsor Prize Pack, (the "Prize"), you must attend the UWindsor International Open Day on Friday, June 4, 2021 and solve and find all of the scavenger hunt items identified in the UWindsor Virtual International Open Day Scavenger Hunt.
  2. Contest entrants will receive one entry to the random draw when the entrant finds all the scavenger hunt items identified in the UWindsor Virtual International Open Day Scavenger Hunt.
  3. Entries will be accepted on June 4, 2021 between 8:30 am and 11:30 am EDT online only at the UWindsor Virtual International Open Day event.
  4. The winner of the draw will be randomly drawn from the pool of all entries who have successfully identified all of the scavenger hunt items for UWindsor Virtual International Open Day online event.
  5. The draw will take place on June 7, 2021.?
  6. The winner will receive notification of the award to the email address they provide when they register to attend the event.
  7. Participants must answer a skill testing question to receive their prize.?
  8. The Prize will be delivered by mail to the recipient.
  9. The Prize is non-refundable and non-transferrable and cannot be exchanged.
  10. Contest entrants agree that the University of Windsor shall be permitted to publish the name of the Prize winner.?
  11. The award must be claimed by July 4, 2021.
  12. By participating in this contest, all entrants agree to be bound by these Rules & Restrictions and agree that the University of Windsor may terminate the contest at any time without prior notice or change the Rules & Restrictions applying thereto.?
  13. This contest is governed by Canadian and Ontario law.
