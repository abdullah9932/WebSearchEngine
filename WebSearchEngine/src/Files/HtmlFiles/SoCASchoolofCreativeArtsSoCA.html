Welcome to SoCA

 
The School of Creative Arts (SoCA) offers Undergraduate degree programs in Music; Visual Arts; Visual Arts and the Built Environment (VABE);  and Film Production & Media Arts. Also Graduate Programs (MFA): Visual Arts; and Film and Media Arts, at the University of Windsor.
