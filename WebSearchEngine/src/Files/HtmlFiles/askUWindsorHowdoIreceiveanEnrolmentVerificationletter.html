How do I receive an Enrolment Verification letter?

Proof of enrolment is often required by financial institutions, government agencies, insurance companies or employers. The University of Windsor offers an Enrolment Verification Letter (no charge) and an Enrolment Certificate. 
 
Please note: Please consult the Registrarial Service Fees website for the cost of the Enrolment Certificate. 
 
 
It is up to the student to contact the organization they are dealing with to determine if an Enrolment Verification Letter is sufficient OR if an Enrolment Certificate is required.
 
Students are able to generate their own Enrolment Verification Letter in UWinsite Student free of charge if they are registered in classes. These letters confirm:
  • Current registration
  • Year and program of study
  • Full- or Part-time status
  • Eligibility to enroll in an upcoming session
 
To Print or Download your Enrolment Verification Letter through UWinsite Student:
  1. In your web browser, preferably Google Chrome, go to?www.uwindsor.ca/uwinsitestudent. 
  2. Click the blue?SIGN IN TO UWINSITE STUDENT?button. 
  3. On the Sign in screen, enter (or select) your UWinID@uwindsor.ca. Click the Next button. 
  4. On the Enter password screen, enter your UWin Account password. Click the Sign in?button. 
  5. On the Student Homepage in UWinsite Student, click the My Academics tile.
  6. Click Self Service Letters in the left navigation. 
  7. In the Select the Letter to Print drop-down menu, select “Enrolment Verification Letter”.
  8. In the Select Desired Term drop-down menu, select the term for which you need an Enrolment Verification Letter.
  9. Click Submit.
  10. UWinsite Student will generate your Enrolment Verification Letter. This may take a minute or so. You’ll see a small   icon in the upper right corner while the system is working. Once generated, your Enrolment Verification Letter will open in a new tab or window.   
  11. NOTE: you may need to turn off your web browser’s pop-up blockers.
  12. Once your Enrolment Verification Letter is generated, you can download or print it.
 
Please note: if you are extending your study permit application, please do not use the enrollment verification from UWinsite Student. You are instructed to request the enrolment certificate from ask.uwindsor.ca and ask for an enrollment certificate with the program completion date listed on it.
 
To learn more about Enrolment Certificates and how to get one, please read this ask.UWindsor.ca
