Available and Remote Campus Services

A greater number of in-person services are available on-campus to support the University of Windsor community. Please see below for a list of in-person and remote services and their availability.

UPDATED: March 29, 2022

BLUE: Open for in-person (or by appointment in-person) service

GOLD: Open for remote service

GREY: In-person service is closed

Open for remote serviceIn-person service is closed

CAW Student Centre

Open for in-person service?

  • The Pharmacy, Dentist and other third-party vendors within CAW Student Centre are available by appointment.
  • Service availability (in-person or remote) for businesses and services within may vary.
  • Precautionary measures remain in place. Visitors must be prepared to show a green badge on the Safe Lancer application. Safety Ambassadors are stationed at the entrance to assist with QR code scanning.

Campus Community Police 

Open for remote serviceIn-person service is closed

  • Campus Community Police operate every day 24/7 and can respond to any matter that requires assistance. There is an officer stationed at all times at the Campus Community Police Office. Signs are posted on the front door indicating how to contact Campus Community Police should the need arise.
  • Phone:  
    • 519-253-3000 ext. 4444 for emergency issues  
    • 519-253-3000 ext 1234 for non-emergency issues 
    • 911 at any time  
      • (dialing 911 from your cell phone will go directly to the Windsor Police 911 Communications Centre) 
      • (dialing 911 from a University landline or campus emergency phone will go directly to the Campus Community Police Dispatch Centre)
    • If the door to the office should be locked, a call box is located by the door. Pressing the button will put you directly in touch with an officer.
  • Webpage: https://www.uwindsor.ca/campuspolice/

Career Development & Experiential Learning

Open by appointment for in-person serviceOpen for remote service

  • Email: careerservices@uwindsor.ca
  • Webpage: www.uwindsor.ca/cdel
  • Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:30 am-4:30pm
  • Book appointments, register for online career workshops, and search for job opportunities via mySuccess.
  • Most career advising appointments are taking place online, but a limited number of in-person appointments with a career advisor or consultant are available. Career exploration and job search questions can be emailed to careerservices@uwindsor.ca.
  • Find upcoming career events, online peer advising hours, and employer networking opportunities on the mySuccess Career Events calendar.
  • Students and supervisors with questions about the Ignite Work Study or VIP-Community Service Learning programs should continue to communicate with coordinators at ignite@uwindsor.ca and vip@uwindsor.ca respectively.

Student Counselling Services  

Open for remote serviceIn-person service is closed

  • Open for virtual phone or video appointments. If you wish to make an appointment, we are working remotely by offering telephone and/or video conferencing.   
  • Please email scc@uwindsor.ca and we will contact you back within 24-48 hours.  
  • If you need to speak with someone immediately please contact MySSP at 1-844-451-9700 or download the ‘MySSP’ app for 24/7 talk or text counselling by licenced counsellors in multiple languages and regardless of geographical location. 
  • If your or someone else’s life is at risk, please call 911 for emergency services.
