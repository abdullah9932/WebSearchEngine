Transforming Windsor Law Donor Levels

By donating to the Transforming Windsor Law campaign, you are supporting the upcoming renovations to the Ron W. Ianni Faculty of Law building. With your help, we are going to take what’s good about our building and make it more warm, welcoming and suitable for law teaching today. Your investment in the building will enhance our reputation and make us all proud. 

To make your contribution to Windsor Law or Transforming Windsor Law, please contact Karen Momotiuk Chapman (karenm@uwindsor.ca) or Cory Simard (Cory.Simard@uwindsor.ca).

President's Circle - Gifts of $50,000+

Recognition awarded:

  • Recognition on donor wall
  • Naming of physical space with appropriate gift level
  • Two tickets to an exclusive UWindsor event
  • Group recognition on the UWindsor website at the President’s Circle gift level
  • Inclusion in the annual report to donors
  • Acknowledgment through social media
  • Invitation to presidential events
  • Inclusion in our Transforming Windsor Law campaign thank-you publication
Chancellor's Circle - Gifts of $25,000+

Recognition awarded:

  • Recognition on donor wall
  • Two tickets to an exclusive UWindsor event
  • Group recognition on the UWindsor website at the Chancellor’s Circle gift level
  • Inclusion in the annual report to donors
Bruce P. Elman Circle - Gifts of $15,000+

Gifts of $100+

Gifts of $100+ will receive recognition in our annual donor report, published in the summer issue of our alumni Access newsletter.
