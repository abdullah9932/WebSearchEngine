Undergraduate Research

 

Outstanding Scholars

The Outstanding Scholars Program provides our top Science students with unique opportunities for academic discovery in research, mentorship by faculty and scholarship funding. Click here to learn more about the Outstanding Scholars Program.

 

UWill Discover is an annual undergraduate research conference that provides students the opportunity to present their research to the faculty and their peers on the UWindsor Campus. Consider participating in UWill Discover!

Undergraduate Participation in Research

 

With a 1:20 faculty to student ratio, more than 65% of our undergraduate Science students participate in research.

This is one of the best undergraduate research participation rates in Ontario! Whether in the field or in the lab, you can participate in undergraduate research for credit or as a volunteer.
