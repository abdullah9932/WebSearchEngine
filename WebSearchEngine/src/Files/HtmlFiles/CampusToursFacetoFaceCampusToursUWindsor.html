Covid-19 Health & Safety Alert

The health and safety of our community is a top priority. Given that the Windsor-Essex Region moves into the Red Zone of the Provincial Covid-19 Framework, effective November 30th we will halt in-person services.

We are still here for you – virtually! The Recruitment team offers many ways to connect. Please connect with us, using the option that suits you best at the link below. We look forward to introducing ourselves virtually, and showing you why we are Windsor Proud! When it is safe to do so, we look forward to welcoming you to campus!

Connect Virtually
  • All guests must register in advance
    All guests must register in advance for their tour (providing a minimum of 48 hours notice) and each timeslot will allow a maximum of 10 visitors. Registered tour participants may bring 1 guest – unfortunately, walk-ins will not be permitted.
  • All Guests Must Wear a Mask, Pass Safety Screening and Be Vaccinated

    All visitors will be required to wear a non-medical mask while on campus, and must show successful completion of a ‘green’ badge from the Safe Lancer App COVID 19 Self-Assessment Tool (Apple Store Download here, Google Play Download here), or complete a printable version of the questionnaire upon arrival.

    Effective October 7th, 2021,all students, faculty, staff and visitors coming onto campus must be fully vaccinated. Please complete the vaccine attestation form for yourself and your guest before you arrive on campus. Each attendee will need to complete this form separately and can be found here!

    To complete the Attestation Form, follow these steps:
    1. What is your primary association with the University of Windsor? Select “Visitor”
    2. How frequently will you visit the university campus during Fall semester? Select “Infrequently”
    3. Enter personal information
    4. Input your vaccination information
    5. Add the code you receive upon completion to the Safe Lancer App

    Once you have completed the form you will receive a code to enter into the Safe Lancer App.

    Visitors who are fully vaccinated that do not have the technology to complete the vaccine attestation form or those who do not wish to upload their vaccination records into our database can have their proof of ID and Vaccination Records validated by one of our Health & Safety staff and receive a UWindsor Proof of Vaccination Card. This card can be repeatedly used, however, successful completion of a ‘green’ badge from the Safe Lancer App COVID 19 Self-Assessment Tool will be required upon each individual visit to campus.

    To receive a UWindsor Proof of Vaccination Card, visit us on campus:
    Katzman Lounge - Vanier Hall
    Monday - Friday
    Walk-In 8:00am - 11:00am
    Appointments - Call (519) 253-3000 ext. 4549/4548 from 1:00pm - 4:00pm
  • Your Campus Experience
    Once you arrive on campus, you’ll be taken on atour route by a Student Ambassador, showing the highlights of our riverside campus.At this time we have indoor building access for our Student Centre, Human Kinetics, Engineering, Business and Library facilities.Following your tour, you'll have an opportunity to connect with one of our Recruitment specialists, should you have any remaining questions.

    Please note that tours on Saturday's will not offer access to any facilities or advising from our Recruitment specialists.
  • Tour Times
    Tours are offered at 10:30 am & 2:30 pm Wednesday through Friday, and most Saturdays at 1:00 pm.
  • After booking your tour
    After booking your tour you will receive communications with full details on what to expect through email.
