The Open Page

Welcome to the UWindsor Faculty of Education Open Page, a collection of online teaching resources and how-tos about open and online education.

The links below connect to key UWindsor pedagogical and technological supports for effective online teaching. For a collection of lab/simulation resources for STEM classes, see this Google form. Our own student-led Tool Parade of videos and podcasts overview a variety of digital tools and platforms, overviewing classroom uses, student data implications, and differentiated learning possibilities, while the Further Resources videos below explore some core issues in online education.
