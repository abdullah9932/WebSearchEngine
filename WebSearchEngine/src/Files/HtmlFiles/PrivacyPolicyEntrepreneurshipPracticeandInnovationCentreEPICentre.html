Privacy Policy

The authorization for the collection of this information is the University of Windsor Act, 1962. The collection of this information is compliant with the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act of the Province of Ontario. The purpose of this data collection is related directly to, and needed by, the University of Windsor to administer the relationship between EPICentre and its students/partners/stakeholders, and to adhere to governmental reporting requirements. Data collected will be used and disclosed as follows:

  • for mentoring/advising services;
  • where the operational necessity of the University requires the disclosure;
  • federal and provincial government offices and Ministries for funding, statistical analysis and planning purposes;
  • may be used for academic research studies;
  • personal information may be used in aggregate reporting in EPICentres promotional materials;
  • for evaluation of programs and services offered by EPICentre; and
  • as required by statute or law
