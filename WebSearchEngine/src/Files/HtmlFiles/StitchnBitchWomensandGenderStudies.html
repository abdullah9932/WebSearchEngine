Stitch 'n Bitch

Friday, April 8, 2022 - 14:00 to 16:00

Women's and Gender Studies is hosting a Stitch 'n Bitch open to all students

riday, April 8

2:00 - 4:00pm

April 8 in Odette 123

Please bring whatever crafts you are presently working on or come to learn how to crochet/knit.  Stitch 'n Bitch groups have been around since World War II and have created safe spaces to discuss current issues and produce crafts. 

Register online

If you are experiencing financial difficulty and are in need of knitting/crochet supplies, please contact wgst@uwindsor.ca.
