Mission Statement

The Office of Institutional Analysis advances the strategic goals of the university by providing leadership and direction for institutional data and analyses.

What We Do

  • Provide comprehensive analyses, research, and guidance for evidence-based decision-making, institutional assessment, policy formulation, and strategic planning.
  • Facilitate the interpretation and insightful use of institutional data.
  • Build relationships with government to advance the University’s strategic priorities.
