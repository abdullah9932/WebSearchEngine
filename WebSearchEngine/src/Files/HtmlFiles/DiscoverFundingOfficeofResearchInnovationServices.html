UWindsor Funding Opportunities

The University of Windsor offers a variety of funding opportunities for small researcher projects. These grants are aimed at assisting emerging researchers as they establish themselves, helping them pursue larger funding opportunities, and funding research assistants to help carry out work. Grants also fund conference travel and knowledge mobilization activities for researchers at all career stages. Internal grant programs include:

  • Research Grants for Women
  • Explore Grants for Small Research Projects
  • Exchange-Travel Grants
  • Undergraduate Research Experience
  • NSERC/SSHRC General Research Fund
  • Schulich-UWindsor Opportunities for Research Excellence
  • Tri-Success Grants

Learn more about UWindsor Internal Funding
