Campus Pride Centre // Open Mic Night

February 16, 2017 @ 7:00 pm - 9:00 pm

  • « Floetry: Women & Intersectionality
  • UWSA Office Closed »

Join Jack.org UWindsor and The Campus Pride Centre for a LGBTQA+ and mental health themed open mic night!

This event promises to be a fun and supportive night for channeling your inner creativity, or just sitting back and listening to some amazing works of art. refreshments and snacks will be provided!

If you know in advance you would like to perform, please email us at jackorg@uwindsor.ca and let us know what you will be performing so we can add you to the line up! Performers will also we able to sign up at the event!
