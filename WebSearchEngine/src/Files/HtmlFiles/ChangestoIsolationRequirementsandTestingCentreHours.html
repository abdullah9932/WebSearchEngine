Changes to Isolation Requirements
Based on current federal, provincial and local trends, the University of Windsor is aligning with public health directives for positive and/or presumed cases and close contacts. Effective Monday, April 4, 2022, the University of Windsor will move to the provincial standard of five-day isolation for positive and/or presumed COVID-19 cases and certain close contacts as outlined in provincial guidance.

Illness reporting for students, faculty and staff is required via email to covid19reporting@uwindsor.ca. Specific instructions regarding campus return date for individuals who have reported COVID-19 related illness, close contact or travel will be provided directly as part of this process.

The Guidance for COVID-19 Positive, Presumed Positive and Close Contacts document outlines important steps when reporting an illness.

Screening through the daily self-assessment questionnaire (Safe Lancer App) continues to be required.
