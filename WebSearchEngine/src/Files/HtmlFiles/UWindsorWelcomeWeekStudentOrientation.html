UWindsor Welcome Week

PLEASE NOTE:Details about Welcome Week 2022 will be available soon, so be sure to check back later!

Thank you to everyone who attended Welcome Week this year. We hope that you were able to learn more about your UWindsor journey, take part in some awesome events and meet some new friends along the way.

2021 WINNERS - Team Safari Shareholders

The Safari Shardholders, from Business, swept the virtual competition this year and took home the top prize of The Shield and bragging rights! 

What is Welcome Week?

Welcome Week is the time for you to become a Lancer! It is a period that encompasses all of the different events planned for incoming students. The events are completely student-run, supported by staff, and are mainly coordinated by the Student Success and Leadership Centre, though other campus partners do host their own events as well. Welcome Week counts on our student staff, as well as the Faculty Leaders who proudly represent their Lancer spirit to that ensure incoming and returning students have the most entertaining and welcoming first week of their semester! 

Why attend Welcome Week?

  • Free prize giveaways

  • New friendships

  • Long-lasting memories

  • An opportunity to become acquainted with faculty, staff, and student leaders

  • The chance to be part of a UWindsor tradition

  • Learn valuable lessons from senior students

  • Take a positive first step towards your University career
