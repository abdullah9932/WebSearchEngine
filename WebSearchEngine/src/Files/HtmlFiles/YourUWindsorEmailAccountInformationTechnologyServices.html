Your UWindsor Email Account

Unsure of your current email platform?

Outlook

UWindsor students, faculty, staff, alumni and retirees use Outlook for their UWinID@uwindsor.ca email.

Sign in to Outlook

Gmail

The migration of student and active alumni email accounts from?UWin?Gmail to Outlook was completed March 2021. Please note, Google Drive access is only available until April 30, 2022. The accounts will start being deleted after that date as part of the Google Services Retirements. Sign in here for Gmail.

For alumni looking to reactivate their alumni email account, please see Alumni Email Services.
