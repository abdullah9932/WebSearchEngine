Description

LambtonSafe is an essential tool to enhance your safety at Lambton College. The app will send you important safety alerts and provide instant access to campus safety resources. LambtonSafe is the official mobile safety app of the Lambton College.

LambtonSafe benefits include:

- Safety notifications: Receive instant notifications and instructions from campus safety when on-campus emergencies occur.

- Emergency help: contact campus safety staff quickly for help in an emergency.

- Campus safety resources: access all important Lambton College safety resources in one convenient app.

Thousands of students, faculty, and staff at the Lambton College are already using LambtonSafe. Download today and ensure that you're prepared in the event of an emergency.
