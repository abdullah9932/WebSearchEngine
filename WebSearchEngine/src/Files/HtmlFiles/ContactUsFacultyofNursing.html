Contact Us

The Faculty of Nursing is located on the 3rd floor of the Anthony P. Toldo Health Education Centre (HEC),  on Sunset Avenue (just south of Fanchette St.) between Wyandotte Street West and University Avenue West. 

Please take the elevators or east-side stairwell (closest to the California Street entrance) to the third floor to enter the nursing main office. Do not take the stairwell on the west-side (Sunset entrance) to get to the 3rd floor, as this 3rd floor entrance is locked at all times and is only accessible for those with card reader access.

Office Hours: 

Monday to Friday from 8:30 a.m to 4:30 p.m.
