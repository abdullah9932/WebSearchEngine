Arrival - Early
  • Residence Services will not be accepting Early Arrivals for any of the Inter/Summer 2022 sessions
     
  • Visit our website for additional Inter/Summer 2022 move-in information at https://www.uwindsor.ca/residence/1285/applying-residence
     
  • If you’re moving in after the scheduled move-in date for the session you applied for, you will need to submit a late arrival form. 
     
  • To submit a late arrival form, visit:  https://uwindsor.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_eWcYYKCOElDhRqJ 


    Updated:  March 21, 2022
Arrival - Late

Late Arrivals – Inter/Summer 2022 

  • Residence Services is accepting late arrival requests for Inter/Summer 2022 sessions for new incoming residence students 

  • Residence students who are arriving after their scheduled Inter/Summer 2022 session move-in date, must submit a Late Arrival Form 

  • For scheduled move-in dates, visit https://www.uwindsor.ca/residence/1285/applying-residence 

  • Residence students who have been approved for a late arrival, will only be able to move-in Monday through Friday, between 9:00 am and 4:00 pm 

  • Late Arrivals/Move-in's will not take place on Saturdays or Sundays 

  • Residence students will receive an email confirming their late arrival  

  • If you arrive pre/post as listed above, you will need to find alternate accommodations until the Residence Office re-opens 

    • Click below for Late Arrival Form

      • https://uwindsor.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_eWcYYKCOElDhRqJ 

 

Updated:  March 21, 2022

Service Animals
  • If you are supported by a service animal, please visit the Student Accessibility Services website, https://www.uwindsor.ca/studentaccessibility/ to discuss the intake process to register your service animal for accommodation.
     
  • To learn more about the general behavioural expectations of service animals, common situations to anticipate or to access the University of Windsor Policy on Presence of Service Animals on Campus, please visit: http://www.uwindsor.ca/ohrea/166/service-animals
